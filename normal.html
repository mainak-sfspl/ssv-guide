Sampurna Vault â€“ Normal User Guide Secure Secret Management for Employees 1. Introduction Welcome to the Sampurna Vault . This is a secure, encrypted platform where you can safely store your sensitive data (passwords, API keys, certificates, confidential notes). Unlike saving passwords in Excel or Notepad, saving them here ensures they are encrypted and accessible only to you . Prerequisites Before you begin, please install an Authenticator App on your mobile phone. You will need this for Two -Factor Authentication (2FA). Google Authenticator (Android / iOS) Microsoft Authenticator (Android / iOS) 2. First -Time Setup (Mandatory) You only need to do this once . Step 1: Access the Vault 1. Open your web browser (Chrome/Edge recommended). 2. Go to the Vault URL: https://4.187.152.147:8200 (Note: If you see a "Not Secure" warning because of the self -signed certificate, click Advanced -> Proceed ). Step 2: Login via OIDC 1. On the Vault login screen, look at the Method dropdown. 2. Select OIDC (Do not use Token or LDAP). 3. Click the Sign in with OIDC Provider button. Step 3: Authenticate & Set New Password 1. You will be redirected to the Sampurna Secure Login page (Dark Theme). 2. Username: Enter your Employee ID (e.g., 100015). 3. Password: Enter the temporary password provided by IT: 1234 Step 4: Configure OTP (2FA) 1. After giving your temporary password, you will see a QR Code on the screen. 2. Open your Authenticator App on your phone. 3. Tap + or Add Account -> Scan QR Code . 4. Scan the code on the screen. 5. Your phone will generate a 6 -digit code. Enter that code into the browser. Step 5: Set New Password and Give your Email 1. Update Password: The system will immediately ask you to change your password. a. Enter 1234 as the current password. b. Enter a strong new password (at least 8 characters). c. Confirm the new password and submit. 2. Give Your Email and submit. Success! You are now logged into Vault. 3. Daily Usage Guide How to Login Daily- 1. Go to https://4.187.152.147:8200 . 2. Select Method: OIDC . 3. Click Sign in . 4. Enter your Employee ID and New Password . 5. Enter the 6-digit OTP from your mobile app. How to Access Your Secret Folder- Once logged in, you will see a list of "Secret Engines". 1. Click on ssv (Sampurna Secure Vault). 2. Click on users . 3. Find and click on your Employee ID (e.g., 100015). 4. Click on the folder named mysecret . a. Note: This is your private workspace. No other employee can see inside this folder. How to Create a New Secret 1. Inside your mysecret folder, click + Create Secret (top right). 2. Path for this secret: Give it a name (e.g., facebook -password or project -api-keys). 3. Secret Data: a. Key: (e.g., username) | Value: (e.g., john.doe@email.com ) b. Key: (e.g., password) | Value: (e.g., MySuperSecretPass123!) 4. Click Save . How to Retrieve/View a Secret 1. Navigate to your folder (ssv -> users -> ID -> mysecret). 2. Click on the secret name (e.g., facebook -password). 3. Click the Eye Icon ( ) next to the password to reveal it. 4. Click the Copy Icon ( ) to copy it to your clipboard. 4. Forgot Password / Lost Phone? Since this system is highly secured with encryption, there is no automatic "ForgotPassword" link on the login page. Scenario A: You Forgot Your Password- 1. Send an email to the Sampurna IT Admin Helpdesk . a. Subject: Vault Password Reset Request - [Your Employee ID] 2. The IT Admin will reset your account. 3. Your password will be reset to the default (1234). 4. You must follow the First -Time Setup steps again to set a new password. Scenario B: You Lost Your Phone / Authenticator App- 1. Contact the Sampurna IT Helpdesk immediately. 2. Request an "OTP Reset" . 3. The admin will clear your 2FA settings. 4. On your next login, you will be prompted to scan a New QR Code to set up the authenticator on your new device.
